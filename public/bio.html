Our History

Battleheart was founded by a mutual desire to give the people good fun music that would speak to their soul and bodies. Since their initial high school days where philosophy and social justice classes drew them more than math and science, Tommy, Luke, and Milan have never stopped wanting to learn more, have pushed themselves into new territories musically (and otherwise!), and have continuously challenged each other to improve their joint artistic creation.
